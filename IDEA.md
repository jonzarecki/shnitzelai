# שניצל.ai — Schnitzel.AI

**Date:** March 13, 2026
**Authors:** Jonathan Zarecki, Matan Kalp, Yoav Halperin

## Concept

An AI-powered humor website that generates schnitzel-themed versions of current news and world events. Think "The Onion" meets AI image generation — but everything is schnitzel.

## How It Works

1. The site takes real, trending news topics (politics, world events, pop culture)
2. An AI model reimagines them as schnitzel-themed content — images, headlines, or short articles
3. The output is absurdist humor: current events presented as (or through the lens of) schnitzels

### Example Topics Discussed

- Trump headlines → schnitzel form
- Maduro / Venezuela → schnitzel form
- Any breaking news → schnitzel form

The phrase that captures it: **"שניצל בצורה של החדשות"** — schnitzel in the form of the news.
